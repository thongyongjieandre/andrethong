About

Who I Am

I am a Senior ICT&SS Policy Specialist at GovTech Singapore, leading third-party risk management and policy reform at the intersection of governance and technology.

My career spans national Critical Information Infrastructure — from governing Land Transport CII resilience at CSA to authoring enterprise-wide frameworks at SPH Media aligned to NIST CSF 2.0, CCoP 2.0, and PCI-DSS v4.0 in OSCAL format.

I believe in Policy as Code — making governance machine-readable, verifiable, and developer-friendly. I integrate AI into daily workflows and champion practical, ship-it innovation.

Engineering graduate of Nanyang Technological University. Fluent across NIST, ISO 27001, SOC 2, and Singapore's regulatory landscape.
